3 Must-Try Breathing Techniques

4-4-4-4 Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, and hold again for 4 seconds.

Best for: Stress, Anxiety, Focus

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, and exhale slowly for 8 seconds.

Best for: Relaxation, Sleep, Reducing Anger

Nostril Breathing

Close the right nostril, inhale through the left. Close the left nostril, exhale through the right, then inhale through the right.

Best for: Balancing energy, Calming nerves
